Why Should You Use Facebook Ads For Your Business?

With over 2 billion active accounts, Facebook has one of the most biggest reaches of any social media platform out there. What’s more, Facebook advertising platform allows you to show your adverts to customers based upon incredibly niche interests and behaviors, which results in a more effective use of your advertising budget.

This means that you can get your product or service in-front of your most relevant customers for a fraction of the cost of traditional advertising methods such as TV, Radio, and billboards.

Whether your business is looking for a Facebook ads agency to advertise on them for you, or just require an agency to manage them on a day to day level for you, I am here to work with you.

How Are The Hours Used?

The management fees shown above covers the whole suite of BaseOne Digital’s marketing services, and comes with the first month free as a revocable ‘trial period’. The packages are available in 3 different tiers, with incremental access to more digital marketing services. The fee will pay for a certain amount of hours per month which can be allocated towards a wide variety of different marketing services. Together, we can decide on the best strategy to help your business expand online.

For example, if you wanted to focus on increasing your website’s Google search engine rankings first then the majority of your available hours per month would go into SEO. Whereas, if you wanted to expand your reach on social media, your hours would be distributed into the creation of social media advertising campaigns. It’s totally flexible towards your small business needs and goals.

To give you greater transparency, you’ll receive a monthly report of the tasks that have been completed, along with the results from the previous month and the next steps for the following month. The retainer is charged on a monthly basis so you can cancel at anytime, I don’t believe in getting tied down in long-drawn out contracts which means that the power is in your hands.
